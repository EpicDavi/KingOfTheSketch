Welcome to a drawing version of King of the Hill!

The Game is Simple:

  • When you aren't the peasant or the King, you can vote on which sketcher has a better drawing depending on how similiar your drawing is to the theme!
  • When you are the peasant, be ready to draw great because it's your chance to beat the King!
  • Kings can't last forever! If the peasant gets more votes than the King, the peasant becomes the King!
  • When you become the King, compete with the other sketchers to win as many games as possible!
  • Challenge your friends and beat each others scores.
