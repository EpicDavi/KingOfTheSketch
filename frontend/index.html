Welcome to a drawing version of King of the Hill!

The Game is Simple:

  • When you join in, you can see what position you are in the queue to draw.
  • During this time, you can vote on your favorite or most-theme-resemblind drawing and that artist gets more ink to draw with!
  • The King will try to stay King for as long as he can, but if the peasant beats him in votes when the time runs out, the peasant will win and become the King!
  • Don't worry if you lose, because you can still vote for your favorite and before you know it, you will be drawing again!
  • Challenge your friends and beat each others scores and see how you do on the leaderboard!
